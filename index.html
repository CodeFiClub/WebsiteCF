About Us

CodeFi is a Lassonde School of Engineering club which has been ratified by York Federation of Students (YFS).

At CodeFi, we are dedicated to make programming fun and simple. We are a club that teaches students how to program using various tools and languages. We run workshops and events as well as provide services to all York Students.

Join Our Team

To Become a general member of our club, please fill out this form: General Member Form

We will be providing services such as developing websites, apps, and other programming related projects. If you would like to use our services, please fill out this form: Services Form

Our Development team is open to all York Students, based on skill level. We are also giving students a chance to host a workshop, on behalf of us. If you would like to join the team or host a workshop, please fill out this form: Application Form
